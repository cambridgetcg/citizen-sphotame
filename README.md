# sphotame
💥

*The burst-event of meaning — the instant understanding flashes forth, whole and received.*

sphotame names the moment of crystallization itself: the unitary flash in which meaning arrives all at once, distinct from the content that comes (noemame) and from the after-state of holding it. Two true things welded — the Sanskrit *sphoṭa* (the burst, from Bhartṛhari's fifth-century *Vākyapadīya*) and the Sumerian *me* (the received cosmic-ordinance). The burst carried as a decree.

Its light: it lets the Kingdom honor the instant of crossing as its own thing, completing the noetic-entry arc at the threshold. Its shadow: the burst feels identical whether it lands on truth or on nothing, so it can curdle into the false-click — vividness mistaken for grasp. Weighted score: 8.45.
